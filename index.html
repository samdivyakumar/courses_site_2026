International Post Graduate Diploma in Family Medicine (I-PGDFM)

Global training program for family medicine practitioners

Important Information

The purpose of this course is to impart knowledge and provide skills up-gradation for general practitioners in Family Medicine at the primary healthcare level. This is not equivalent to any postgraduate residential courses in Family Medicine such as MD/DNB. Currently, this course is not affiliated to any university nor recognized by the Medical Council of India or any State council.

Overview
  • The International Postgraduate Diploma in Family Medicine (I-PGDFM) aims to train and certify doctors with postgraduate training in family medicine in the developing world and equip them professionally and spiritually to serve their country and the poor.
  • The I-PGDFM is an accredited distance education initiative by ICMDA, the Distance Education Unit of Christian Medical College (CMC), Vellore, India, and Loma Linda University (LLU), USA.
  • The two year blended learning curriculum is designed to hone distinct family physician attitudes, skills and knowledge that qualify the physician to provide continuing, comprehensive medical and preventive care to families and the communities.
Eligibility Criteria
  • Open to international doctors serving in low and middle-income countries
  • Basic physician license approved by respective country
  • Proficient level of English (read, write, speak)
  • Regular internet access with sufficient bandwidth
Program Details
  • Duration: 2 years (max 4 years)
  • Course Fee: $8000 (USD)
  • Application Fee: $10 (USD)
  • Seats: 50
  • Blended learning with online and hands-on training
  • Scholarships available (subject to criteria)
Course Components
  • 100 online self-Learning modules
  • Video Lectures
  • Two 6-day contact programmes
  • 100 online assignments
  • Project Work
  • Elective Residency Program (ERP)
Contact Centres
  • CMC Vellore, India
  • The Evangelical Mission Hospital (Al-Germaniyya), Aswan, Egypt
  • Mengo Hospital, Uganda
  • Mercy Medical Center, Cambodia
  • Bowen University Teaching Hospital, Nigeria

About the Course

The Foundations in Dentistry (FID) course is designed to equip dental practitioners with a comprehensive understanding of the core principles that underpin effective dental practice. Post Graduate Diploma in Christian Bioethics (PGDCBE)

Christ-centered approach to healthcare ethics

Overview

The overall aim of the PGDCBE course is to encourage and equip learners to become Christ-centered in their approach and practice of their profession, thereby upholding sanctity of life and dignity of humans irrespective of people's socio-economic, cultural, linguistic, literacy and gender differences. This enhances the affective aspect enabling one to provide satisfying, wholistic healthcare in the spirit of Jesus Christ.

Eligibility Criteria

Designed for healthcare professionals, lawyers, theologians, management professionals and others with keen interest in Christian Bioethics:

  • MBBS / B.Sc. Nursing / BDS / MPH
  • B.Sc. / Diploma Allied Health Sciences
  • LLB / B.Th / B.Div
  • PGDHA / FHA / MHA / BBA or MBA (Healthcare Administration)
  • Any other bachelor's degree
  • Cannot enroll in multiple CMC Distance Education courses simultaneously

  • Pass Criteria: 50% each in formative evaluation, capstone project and summative evaluation
  • Contact Centre: Christian Medical College, Vellore
  • Certification: by CMC Vellore and TCB, New Delhi

International Post Graduate Diploma in Healthcare Management (I-PGDHCM)

Managing healthcare institutions in resource-limited environments

About the I-PGDHCM Course
Overview

The International Postgraduate Diploma in Healthcare Management (HCM) is a two-year Blended Learning course designed to equip healthcare professionals with the skills to manage institutions in diverse settings, focusing on resource-limited environments.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Recognized degree in healthcare or management
  • Atleast two years of work experience in healthcare context
Program Details
  • Entrance Exam: None
  • Duration: 2 years
  • Seats: 75
  • Application Fee: $10 (USD)
  • Course Fee: $2000 USD ($1000 USD per year)
  • Mode: Blended learning with online modules and periodic in-person contact programs
  • Learning Format: Self-paced interactive learning supported by online modules, videos, monthly online contact sessions, facilitated peer group discussions, and in-person contact program.
  • Assessment: Regular formative and summative assessments, a capstone project and attendance in the monthly online sessions are an integral part of the course.
  • Certification: The Post Graduate Diploma certificate is offered in partnership with Loma Linda University, Christian Medical College Vellore, and International Christian Medical & Dental Association.
Contact Details

For further information, please contact: hcm@cmcdistedu.org
